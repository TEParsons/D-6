# What is D6? D6 is a Table Top Role Playing Game (TTRPG), like Pathfinder or Dungeons & Dragons. Essentially, the person running the game, the Game Master (GM), describes a scenario and you, the player, decide how your character will respond. You tell the GM what you'd like to do, they ask you to roll some dice to see how successful you are. In a nutshell, that's it! That's the game!
